Layers & Criteria

  • Flood risk – ROR RiskZone (RWS / PDOK)
  • Soil & foundation – BRO Bodemkaart (SGM)
  • Ecology – Natura 2000 areas
  • Cultural heritage – Rijksmonumenten & protected sites
  • Transport – Roads & railways (TOP10NL)
  • Land use – Bestand Bodemgebruik (CBS) & LGN
  • Cadastral – Parcels & boundaries (BRK)
  • Buildings – BAG Panden (incl. usage & build year)
  • Elevation – AHN Digital Surface Model (DSM 0.5m)

Data via PDOK, Rijkswaterstaat, CBS, WUR & Kadaster. Use for regional / quick-scan level, not parcel-level decisions.
